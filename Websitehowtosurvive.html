How To Survive Natural Disasters

How To Survive an Earthquake:

  • Drop, Cover, and Hold On: When you feel the ground shaking, immediately drop to your hands and knees. This position helps prevent you from being knocked over. Cover your head and neck with your arms, and if possible, take cover under a sturdy piece of furniture like a table or desk. Hold on to your shelter until the shaking stops.
  • Stay Indoors: If you are inside, stay there. Do not run outside during the shaking, as falling debris can pose a greater risk. Move away from windows, glass doors, and heavy furniture that could fall.
  • If You Are Outside: Move to an open area away from buildings, trees, streetlights, and utility wires. Stay there until the shaking stops.
  • If You Are in Bed: Stay in bed and cover your head and neck with a pillow. This can help protect you from falling objects.
